"window" objects

1. window.open():
The open() method opens a new browser window.
Tip: Use the close() method to close the window.

2. window.close():
The close() method closes the current window.
Tip: This method is often used together with the open() method.

3. window.moveTo():
Moves a window to the specified position

4. window.resizeTo():
The resizeTo() method resizes a window to the specified width and height.
